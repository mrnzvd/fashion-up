The Brand

At Fashion up, we believe in fashion that doesn't just look good, but also does good for the planet. That's why we're thrilled to unveil our line of sustainable clothing. Our sustainable clothing line is crafted with a meticulous focus on environmental responsibility and ethical production practices. Here are some key features that set us apart:

Materials Matter Timelessness
We source our materials from eco-friendly and sustainable sources, such as organic cotton, bamboo, recycled polyester, and Tencel. By choosing these materials, we reduce our environmental footprint while ensuring the highest quality for our products. We design our clothing to be timeless, versatile pieces that can withstand the test of time. By investing in quality craftsmanship and timeless design, we encourage a shift away from fast fashion and towards a more sustainable approach to dressing.
Minimal Waste Sustainability
From design to production, we prioritize minimizing waste at every stage. We strive to use innovative techniques that reduce fabric waste, recycle materials whenever possible, and opt for packaging that is either biodegradable or recyclable. Sustainability isn't just a buzzword for us—it's at the core of everything we do. With our new line of sustainable clothing, we're taking concrete steps towards a more sustainable future for fashion. But our journey doesn't end here. We are continuously exploring new ways to reduce our environmental impact and improve the sustainability of our products and practices.

Join Us in Making a Difference: We invite you to join us on this journey towards a more sustainable future. By choosing Fashion Up sustainable clothing, you're not just making a fashion statement—you're making a positive impact on the planet and supporting ethical and responsible practices in the fashion industry.

Thank you for your support and keep an eye for our launch at the end of 2024!

The Designer

From my earliest days, the seeds of my passion for fashion and sustainability were sown, nurtured by a deep-rooted love for creativity and transformation. I am Taisa Souza. Long before the term "upcycling" gained traction, I found myself drawn to the art of sewing and reinvention.

Armed with a formal education in Fashion Design and years of practical experience in Retail and Visual Merchandising since my teenage years, I embarked on a journey that seemed predestined. Yet, despite the diverse roles I undertook, a sense of fulfillment remained elusive.

It was amidst the upheaval of the 2020 lockdown that a friend's words resonated deeply within me: "Make your expertise your greatest ally." Inspired by this mantra, I began channeling my passion for sustainability into tangible action, advocating for change within the fashion industry.

However, it was my enduring dream of becoming a stylist that truly ignited the spark for transformation. The vision of individuals adorned in unique creations, radiating confidence and joy, fueled my determination to carve out a sustainable path of my own.

In a serendipitous turn of events, while navigating the intricacies of finance for my full-time job, the seeds of Fashion UP were planted. It became clear to me that I harbored a profound desire to do more—for myself and for the planet. Thus, Fashion UP emerged as a testament to my unwavering commitment to sustainability, with each design meticulously crafted from repurposed denim—a material synonymous with resilience and versatility.

Driven by the sobering environmental toll of clothing production and disposal, I embarked on a mission to breathe new life into discarded denim pieces. By intercepting these materials before they succumb to landfills, Fashion UP seeks to mitigate the adverse effects of fast fashion on our planet.

With every garment produced, we not only minimize waste but also contribute to the preservation of our environment. Each stitch serves as a symbol of our dedication to a more sustainable future—one where fashion and conscience seamlessly intertwine.

At Fashion UP, our ethos is succinct: there is no Planet B. Through our steadfast commitment to upcycling and sustainable practices, we envision a world where fashion becomes a catalyst for positive change—a world where every thread narrates a story of resilience, innovation, and hope.

Join me, Taisa Souza, as we redefine fashion, one stitch at a time. Together, let's transform sustainability from a mere trend into a way of life.

Projects

Stay tuned for updates on the upcoming projects!

By removing litter and debris from these spaces, we take a small but significant step towards improving our environment.

Exchange Market: Join us for a day of clothing exchange! Bring items from your wardrobe that you no longer need and swap them with others. Enjoy music, coffee, and engaging conversations as you participate in this sustainable and community-driven event.

Donations Day: Participate in our clothing donation initiative! Gather clothing items that you no longer use, ensure they undergo proper hygiene processes, and organize them into packs for donation. Your contributions will support institutions in need and make a meaningful difference in the lives of others.
